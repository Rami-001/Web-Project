About Global Compass

Your essential guide to critical services abroad.

Global Compass helps you quickly find hospitals, banks, police stations, pharmacies, and other must-have services so you can focus on the trip, not the logistics.

What we do

  • Keep the directory focused on essential, high-stakes services.
  • Organize everything into clear categories you recognize while traveling.
  • Highlight details that matter abroad: type, area, and how to reach it.
  • Make it easy to scan on a phone when you’re on the move.

What makes us different

  • No endless map of pins or generic tourist listings.
  • Safety-critical places are never buried under ads or noise.
  • Language-light, icon-friendly layout for international use.
  • Designed to grow into shared tools for trips and teams.

How Global Compass fits into your trip

  1. Before you go: Check the availability of hospitals, banks, and police stations near where you plan to stay.
  2. On arrival: Pick a category and see the most relevant options in seconds.
  3. During the trip: Keep key services in mind for changes of plan or emergencies.
  4. Afterward: Reuse your knowledge for future trips or share it with people traveling to the same city.

What’s next

  • Deeper coverage for more cities and neighborhoods.
  • Richer context around each service, tailored to travelers.
  • Saved “trusted places” you can carry across trips.

Future direction

  • Shared views for families, friends, and teams on the same route.
  • Smarter suggestions based on region, time of day, and need.
  • Offline-friendly experiences for weak or unstable connections.
