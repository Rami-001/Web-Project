Frequently Asked Questions

What is Global Compass?

Global Compass is a travel directory platform that helps you find essential services worldwide—hospitals, banks, restaurants, hotels, and more—organized by city and category.

How do I search for services?

Navigate to the Services page, select your country and city, choose a category (Health, Finance, or Lodging), and browse verified service listings with contact info.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for basic browsing. However, creating an account lets you save favorites and access premium features like offline downloads.

What are the available plans?

We offer a free Basic plan with limited searches, and a premium Enterprise plan with unlimited access, priority support, and advanced filtering options.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal data. We never sell your information to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Do you cover emergency services?

Yes! We include hospitals, police stations, and medical volunteers. However, for true emergencies, always call local emergency numbers first (911, 999, etc).
