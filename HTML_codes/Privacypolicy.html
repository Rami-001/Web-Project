Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Travel preferences, saved locations, favorite services
  • Location Data: Approximate location to show relevant local services (with your permission)
  • Usage Data: Services viewed, search queries, interaction with our platform

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and general location data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Log data (pages visited, time spent, features used)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize travel service recommendations
  • Display relevant services based on your location
  • Save your favorite hospitals, banks, and restaurants
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important updates about service availability
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting services, analytics
  • Emergency Situations: If required by law or to protect vital interests
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale

4. Data Security

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal information within our organization
  • Secure authentication methods

5. Your Rights

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw location permissions at any time
  • Export your saved favorites and preferences

6. Location Services

We request location access to:

  • Show nearby emergency services (hospitals, police stations)
  • Display relevant banks, ATMs, and currency exchanges
  • Recommend local restaurants and accommodations
  • Provide accurate travel directions

You can disable location services in your device settings at any time.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

Email: privacy@globalcompass.com
Address: Global Compass Ltd., Digital Street, Beirut, Lebanon
